About Me

My name is Lauren and I am a music producer and singer with a passion for crafting sounds that resonate across Pop, Indie Pop/Rock, and country music landscapes! With a strong foundation in these genres, I bring versatility to my work, also diving into R&B and House music when the atmosphere calls for it. My goal is to create authentic and memorable music that connects with listeners, blending genres and styles to deliver fresh, genre-bending tracks that feel both familiar and innovative. Whether I'm behind the mixing board or the mic, I bring a dynamic and unique energy to each project. At the University of Miami's Frost School of Music, I am a member of the Music Production department, earning a degree in Media, Scoring, and Production with an emphasis in Contemporary Voice and a minor in Music Industry by 2027!
